### Insurance Agency - carries contract with single writers 
### Brokerage - deals with multiple writers(insurance companies)


#### Private & Government Insurance are sharing insurance base it respective province
- Due to conditions in province, and consequent  losses for private insurance firs - they left, therefore government **has** to substitute services of private insurers. 
- In most provinces there is a mix of public & private coverage offerings 

#### MGA - vested with underwriting authority *from* the insurer 
- Also handles function, ordinary executed by an insurer. Biding coverage, underwriting, pricing appointing retail agents within area and settling claims 
- Involved with non-common coverage: professional liability, surplus line of insurance 
- Regular brokerages get in touch with MGAs for out-of-house expertise 

#### Independent agencies - legal entity which contracts with a sponsoring insurance company. Sell products of only one insurer 

#### Independent Brokerage - self explanatory
