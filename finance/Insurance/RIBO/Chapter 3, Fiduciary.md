### Expressed & Implied contracts - written & oral/non-written contracts


#### Parts of the contract:
1. Agreement - meeting of minds regarding terms of contract. Made offer and followed acceptance 
	1. Completing application for insurer - offer. Insurer issues a policy that matched the offer - acceptance 
	2. There is no agreement unless client agrees to send-back conditions 
2. Consideration - legally enforceable contracts **requires** exchange of values. Two parties intend to be bound by contract 
	1. Payment of policy premium, or promise to pay premium later 
3. Legal objects - contract is enforceable only if object has **legal** value. 
4. Legal capacity - **legal person** who enter into the contract 
	1. Brokerages are allowed to sell policy to the minors - if minor is fully aware of the conditions of the contract 
5. Genuine intention - enforceable only if parties had a clear intent to enter into contract 

### Elements of insurance contact 
1. Insurable interest - show that they will suffer financially from loss. 
	1. Ex: son who isn't included in deed of his mother has 0 insurable interest in the house. Although he may inherit it in the future. Unless loss will hurt an entity right now - policy can't be issued 
2. Utmost Good Faith - obligation to comply with terms of contract 
3. Indemnity - restriction to actual amount of insured loss
