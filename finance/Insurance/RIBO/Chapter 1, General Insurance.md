### Risk 
- Object - i buy a car and therefore additional risk 
- Activity - businesses; opening, launching, doing, etc

#### Insurable - pure risk; there is a chance of loss without fin gian
- Speculative risk - with change to gain or lose(gambling, investing, etc)


#### Hazard - condition that increases the chance or severity of risk 
- Physical - observable conditions related to objects of insurance 
	- Example: garage is insurance, however, inside there are chemicals & oil - which are physical hazards 
	- 
- Moral - characteristics of an insured or applicant for insurance that increases likelihood of a loss
	- Mechanic regularly smokes near the chemicals 
	- Carelessness or indifference 

#### Dealing with risk 
- Avoid risk / eliminate it 
- Control / decreasing 
- Retain / accept risk / be prepared to take it
- Transfer / insurance 

#### Types of risk 
1. Financial 
2. Personal 
3. Property 
4. Liability 

- #### Insurance Functions
	- Diversifying/Spreading risk within the pool
	- Credit risk, insurance is required by lenders for big investments/property purchases 
	- Provide protection and "back-up" 
	- IC established measures for loss prevention & reduction, by employing representatives who make an in-person assessment 

#### Reinsurance - insurance for insurance companies. Forms:
- Facultative - transfer of individual risk to reinsurer. With large clients, IC can transfer some risk to reinsurer 
- Treaty - annual portion of portfolio is transferred to reinsurer 


##### Basis:
- Proportional reinsurance - reinsurer receives a prorated share or negotiated % of all policy premiums sold
	- When claim occurs, reinsurer also bears portion of the compensation 
- Non-Proportional - reinsurer *reimburses* for losses over the specific amount
	- Retention limit - threshold 
	- Usually paid an agreed fee
