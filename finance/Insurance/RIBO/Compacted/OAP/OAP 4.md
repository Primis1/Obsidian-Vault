### Section 4 Accident Benefit 

### Listed in Statutory condition 
1. Income replacement - you and other insured person 
2. Caregiver benefit - suffers catastrophic injuries and can't take care of dependent 
3. Non-Earner Benefit - if you don't qualify for IR or CB, and you can't carry on with normal life 
4. Med Benefit - to you or other injured person. Covers excess above other med plans 
5. Rehabilitation Benefit - if not covered by any other plan 
6. Attendant Care Benefit - compensates your or other insured person for aide of attendant. Transportation to accompany insured to/from rehab or med facility - are covered 
7. Payment of other expenses - covers "related" expenses. Visiting insured, repair & replacement of lost items, education or housekeeping expenses - in case of catastrophic injuries 
8. Death benefit - to family member 
9. Funeral Benefit - some last expenses 

### Optional benefit - increase the limit 

- After giving notice to insurer(within 7 days) insured has to file application and send it back to insurer within 30 days 

- DB, Funeral, Other expenses has to be paid within 30 days, other within 10 days (business)

***
#### If you qualify for more than one benefit - you have 30 days to choose 1 of 'em
