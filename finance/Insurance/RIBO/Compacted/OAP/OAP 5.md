### Section 5 Uninsured Automobile 

#### Damage to automobile and content isn't covered, unless driver is identified 
- Loss and Damage coverage is open for purchase 

#### If car is damaged by uninsured but identified driver, insurer will compensate the damage(deductibles apply) 
- If unidentified 25k cap 

Example:
- An accident in Ontario for which an identified but uninsured driver is responsible destroys your $20,000 car, and results in injuries to you and your spouse, totaling $350,000.
	- We will not pay more than the minimum liability limit of $200,000. Of that money, 95%, or $190,000, will go toward payment for bodily injury. The remaining 5%, or $10,000, will apply to the loss of your car.
#### If travelled outside of ON, and injured by uninsured vehicle - no more than min amount in that province will be paid 

#### In case insured wins but can't recover full award amount - insurer fills the difference 
- Subject to limit on coverage 

Time limit to file law suit is 1 or 2 years, for car and other property
