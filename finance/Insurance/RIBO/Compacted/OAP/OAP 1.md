### Insured Responsibilities 
- Promptly notify about material change, or change on the application 
- Inform of any sale or transfer of car interest; except in succession, death or bankruptcy
- In case optional Loss or Damage is purchased - inform about new lien and other insurance against loss 
- Report injury or liability within 7 days after an accident 
- Allow us to inspect a car 

*** 
### Insurer Responsibilities 
1. Pay valid claim within 60 days after proof of loss 
2. Insured may cancel policy at any, with Short rate refund
3. Insurer may cancel policy within 60 days for reason listed on FSRA; after 60 days only through:
	1. non-payment 
	2. false info about vehicle 
	3. knowingly misrepresented 
	4. material change in risk 

Minimum premiums are not refunded.

- Non payment 30-days notice. Insured should pay before last day of notice, otherwise policy will lapse at 12:01 am, local standard time
