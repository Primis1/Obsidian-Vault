### Section 3 - Liability  

### Persona is insured under this policy if he has insured's consent 

#### Insurance limit raises depending on province, insurer will honor legal requirements of the province (from 200k in ON to 500k in AB)

#### If two named insured are suing each other, insurer will defend *each*, to the limit of coverage 

 # * *One day, there is an accident while one is driving and the other is a passenger. Both of them are severely injured as a result of their combined negligence. They sue each other and one is awarded $300,000 and the other $500,000. The combined amount we will pay will not be more than the policy limit of $500,000 plus legal costs and post-judgment interest.


### For leased auto. insurance will pay in order:
1. Who rented a car 
2. Who actually drove a car
3. Who owns a car 

It is used one-by-one until the claim is paid in full 


### Insured reimburses insurer for any payment they weren't liable for
