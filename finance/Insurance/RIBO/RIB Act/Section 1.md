### Definitions:
1. CAI - written document summarizing your insurance coverage. Name & Automobile, purchased coverage, premium charged, time in force
2. Covered/Coverage - something being insured 
3. Direct loss/damage - directly by peril. Damage from collision - DR, car is under repairs(therefore can't be used) - indirect loss. 
4. Excluded driver - someone not specified in the contract to drive in specified car. **In Ontario every person injured in an accident should be compensated**

#### Responsibilities 
1. Inform change in status as driver, or any detail specified in an application 
2. Sale or change in interest of an auto 
	1. Except change in title through succession, death or bankruptcy
3. In an accident with injuries - notice has to be given within **7 days** to your representative(broker, company)

#### Insurer's Responsibilities 
1. Pay claim within 60 days after receiving proof of loss. 
2. If premiums were charged incorrectly = return with interest 
3. If you underpaid - no interest and notice of 60 days

#### Cancellation - after 60 days, insurer, can cancel only under:
1. non-payment 
2. false details about automobile 
3. purposeful misrepresentation 
4. material change in risk 

- Cancellation; notices - will be sent via *methods* and grace **period of 10 or 30 days starts** on the next or after 2 days 
	- To continue coverage person has to pay premium +administrative fees 
	- After 2 non-payments within the same policy - third notice isn't required 
- Cancellation notice - actual notice of cancellation - done, it, over. 
	- If cause is repetitive non-payment - no obligation of grace period is required
